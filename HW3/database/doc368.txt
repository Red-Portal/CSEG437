the samsung galaxy tab is an android-based compact tablet computer produced by samsung[1] that debuted[2] on the 2nd of september at the 2010 ifa in berlin. it features a 7-inch (180 mm) tft-lcd touchscreen, wi-fi capability, a 1.0 ghz arm cortex-a8 ("hummingbird") processor, and the swype input system.[3] it is equipped with a 3.2 mp rear-facing camera and a 1.3 mp front facing camera for video calls.
